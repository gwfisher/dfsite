I remember the Internet circa 1998 where self-hosting things was very common. This was before the plethora of cloud hosting solutions came onto the scene. In fact, everyone small and big self-hosted. And even though we have all these marvelous cloud services like AWS, there will always been something quaint and DIY about self-hosting. As there are more and more grave abuses of privacy and censorship on the modern web, I hope I can encourage people to take the DIY self-hosting route.
